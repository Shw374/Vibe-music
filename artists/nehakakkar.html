Neha Kakkar

About Artist

She first entered the industry with Indian Idol season 2. Her elder sister's presence in the industry helped her with the career and the show. After getting eliminated very early from the show, she focused on playback singing. After Indian Idol, she kick started her journey in the music industry with the album ‘Neha The Rock Star’ composed by Meet Bros in 2008.

In 2013, Kakkar gave her voice in the song ‘Dhating Naach’ for the movie ‘Phata Poster Nikhla Hero’. She gave her voice in the popular song ‘Sunny Sunny’ with Honey Singh in 2014 that conveyed huge fame for the singer. In the same year, Kakkar gave voice in the song ‘London Thumakda’. Then she gave the voice for the song ‘Party Shoes’ of the Kolkata's movie ’Bindass’.

Kakkar played her singing role in the song ‘Magic Mamoni’ of the Bengali movie where acted Mahiya Mahi. She gave singing performance in the song ‘Car Mein Music Baja’. Kakkar played back in the ‘Dilwale’ movie song ‘Tukur Tukur’. Then she gave voice in the song ‘Chitta Kukkad’ for the movie ‘Loveshhuda’. Kakkar continued to sing with performances like song ‘Kala Chashma’ for the movie Baar Baar Dekho in 2016 for the song and song 'Dilbar Dilbar' in 2018 for the film Satyameva Jayate (2018 film), which was recreated by original song with same title from Sirf Tum and became chartbuster.
